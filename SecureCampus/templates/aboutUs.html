About Us

SecureCampus is an advanced surveillance system designed to enhance security and safety in universities, companies, and malls by leveraging cutting-edge technologies. Our system provides real-time monitoring and instant alerts for potential conflicts, inappropriate attire, and unauthorized access, ensuring a secure environment. Developed by a dedicated group of students from the Arab Academy for Science, Technology & Maritime Transport (AASTMT), SecureCampus combines the power of YOLO for rapid object detection and DeepFace for precise facial recognition to deliver unmatched speed and accuracy. Our intuitive interface and comprehensive monitoring capabilities reflect our commitment to harnessing modern technology for creating safer, compliant, and more secure spaces. Our story is rooted in a shared passion for innovation and security, driving us to develop a solution that addresses current challenges and adapts to future needs.

Our Mission

"Our mission is to enhance the security and safety of educational institutions, commercial spaces, and public venues by leveraging cutting-edge artificial intelligence technologies. We aim to provide reliable, real-time surveillance solutions that ensure a secure environment and promote appropriate behavior."

Our Vision

"Our vision is to be the leading provider of intelligent surveillance systems that transform security practices worldwide. We aspire to create a safer and more secure world by pioneering innovative AI solutions that proactively address safety concerns and adapt to the evolving needs of our clients."

Our Goal

"Our goal is to develop and deploy a comprehensive surveillance system that seamlessly integrates face detection, behavior analysis, and compliance monitoring. By utilizing advanced AI models and scalable technologies, we aim to deliver a user-friendly web application that empowers security personnel to respond swiftly and effectively to potential threats, ensuring the safety and well-being of all individuals within the monitored spaces."
